Metadata from the Internet

When enabled, the system will search third-party services for additional metadata about the current song, such as album covers.

What's shared?

Track title, artist, album and system ID. No other information is sent.

Which services are used?

  • Last.fm (lookup only)
  • MusicBrainz (HiFiBerry instance)
  • Cover Art Archive
  • Fanart.tv
  • HiFiBerry MusicDB
